Our Mission

Men For Real Gender Advocacy Group is a grassroots movement committed to protecting the rights of men and boys in South Africa. We advocate for those affected by abuse, neglect, emotional harm, and the silencing of their pain.

We believe true masculinity is not violence or silence ‚Äî but courage, love, healing, and responsibility.

How We Work

Through storytelling, education, emotional healing circles, and community engagement, we work to:

Confront Abuse and Injustice

Addressing abuse and injustice faced by men and boys through advocacy and support.

Create Safe Spaces

Building safe environments for reflection, support, and meaningful dialogue.

Promote Healthy Models

Fostering positive examples of fatherhood and manhood in our communities.

Rebuild Dignity and Purpose

Restoring dignity through spiritual and emotional literacy programs.
